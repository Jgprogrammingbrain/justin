Justin Bieber

From Youtube Sensation to Global Superstar

About Justin

Justin Bieber, born on March 1, 1994, in London, Ontario, Canada, is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to fame as a teen idol. Discovered by talent manager Scooter Braun through YouTube videos in 2008, Bieber quickly gained popularity with his debut EP My World in 2009, followed by his first full-length album My World 2.0 in 2010.

Bieber's music career has been marked by several successful albums, including Believe (2012), Purpose (2015), and Justice (2021). He is known for hit singles like "Baby," "Sorry," and "Love Yourself." Over the years, Bieber has experimented with various musical styles, including pop, R&B, and EDM.

Despite facing controversies and legal issues in the early 2010s, Bieber has continued to evolve as an artist and remains a significant figure in the music industry. He is married to Hailey Baldwin since 2018.
